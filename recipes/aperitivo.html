Aperitivo

The aperitivo is a cocktail typically containing low-alcohol liqueurs and/or vermouths that is intended to open up your palate and stimulate the appetite before a meal.

Let's get started making this drink that is so famously integrated into the Italian culture

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Aperol
  • 1/2 oz blanco tequila
  • 1/2 oz Lillet blanc
  • 1/2 oz bianco vermouth
  • 1 oz orange juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 oz passion fruit puree
  • 5 dashes mango vinegar
  • 5 drops orange flower water
  • 1 pinch Maldon salt
  • Sparkling water, to top
  • Garnish: 1/4 passion fruit
  • Garnish: orange blossom spray

Steps

  1. Add the Aperol, tequila, Lillet blanc, bianco vermouth, orange juice, passion fruit puree, mango vinegar, orange flower water and salt into a shaker with ice and shake vigorously until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into highball glass over fresh ice
  3. Top with sparkling water
  4. Garnish with a quarter of passion fruit
  5. Spritz with orange blossom spray
